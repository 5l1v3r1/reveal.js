Web RTC

This is a developing standard in webpages to get data from your webcam and your microphone.

Once you have a video feed, you can display, alter, and extract information from it using JavaScript.

How To

Use a flick of your hand in mid-air to change the slide.

A two hand swipe up or down activates the slide overview.

Usage Tips

  • Try to keep the rest of your body still.
  • Your hand should preferably be 1 meter from the camera.
  • If it's being over or under sensitive, try varying your distance.
  • Gestures should be performed with open palms facing the camera.
  • To keep unintentional actions to a minimum, try bringing your hands out of sight.

What about Kinect?

Kinect uses a 3D sensor, which costs around $100.

This uses a webcam, which is built into all newer computers. The cheaper ones come at $20 a pop.

However, there are limitations of webcams.

Webcams

Webcams cannot detect depth. It cannot accurately detect position in 3D space.

Webcams can only detect motion. The motion is what I'm using.
